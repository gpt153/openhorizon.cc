## Creating Your First Project

### Step 1: Click "New Project"

From the dashboard, click the **"New Project"** button in the top right.

### Step 2: Fill in Project Details

**Required Fields:**
- **Project Name** - e.g., "Barcelona Student Exchange 2025"
- **Type** - Select from:
  - Student Exchange
  - Teacher Training
  - Vocational Training
  - Youth Exchange
  - Other
- **Location** - Where the project takes place (e.g., "Barcelona, Spain")
- **Start Date** - When the project begins
- **End Date** - When the project ends
- **Number of Participants** - How many students/teachers
- **Total Budget** - Total budget in EUR

**Optional Fields:**
- **Description** - Additional project details
- **Organization** - Your school or institution

### Step 3: Review Auto-Generated Phases

The system automatically creates recommended phases:
1. **Planning** - Initial project setup
2. **Accommodation** - Hotel/hostel booking
3. **Travel** - Flights, trains, buses
4. **Food** - Meals and catering
5. **Activities** - Excursions and educational activities
6. **Insurance** - Travel insurance
7. **Emergency Planning** - Safety procedures
8. **Reporting** - Final documentation

You can:
- ‚úèÔ∏è **Edit** phases to adjust dates, budgets
- ‚ûï **Add** new custom phases
- ‚úñÔ∏è **Skip** phases that don't apply
- üîÑ **Reorder** phases by dragging

### Step 4: Save Your Project

## Tips & Best Practices

### Planning Tips

1. **Start Early** - Begin planning 6-9 months in advance
2. **Use Auto-Populate** - Let the AI suggest based on past projects
3. **Be Specific with AI** - The more details you provide, the better recommendations
4. **Request Multiple Quotes** - Always compare options
5. **Track Expenses Daily** - Don't wait until the end

### Budget Management

1. **Leave Buffer** - Don't allocate 100% of budget
2. **Monitor Health Indicators** - Act on yellow/red alerts
3. **Use Categories** - Makes reporting easier
4. **Accept Quotes Early** - Prices may increase

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "AI is not responding"
- **Solution**: Check your internet connection. AI requires API access.
- **Workaround**: The AI will provide fallback suggestions even offline.

#### "Budget doesn't add up"
- **Solution**: Check for recent expense entries. Refresh the page.
- **Contact**: If persists, contact support with project ID.

#### "Quotes not being received"
- **Check**: Vendor email addresses are correct
- **Check**: Your sent emails (Communications tab)
- **Solution**: Resend or manually contact vendor

#### "Can't edit locked phase"
- **Reason**: Phases lock after acceptance to prevent accidental changes
- **Solution**: Click "Unlock" in phase settings (admin only)

#### "Export not downloading"
- **Check**: Pop-up blocker settings
- **Solution**: Allow downloads from the app domain
- **Alternative**: Try different browser
